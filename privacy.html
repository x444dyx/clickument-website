Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Clickument is built for clarity, evidence, and precision. This policy explains what data is handled and how it is used.

1. Data Collection

Clickument does not collect personal data, browsing history, or external analytics by default. All documentation steps and screenshots are stored locally in the user’s browser unless explicitly exported by the user.

2. Local Storage

Workflow steps, scenario names, and related data are stored using Chrome storage APIs. This may include local storage and/or Chrome sync storage depending on user configuration.

3. Screenshots

Screenshots captured by Clickument are stored locally within the browser environment. They are not transmitted to external servers.

4. Data Export

Users may export documentation data manually. Exported files are controlled entirely by the user. Clickument does not receive or store exported content.

5. Third-Party Services

Clickument does not use third-party tracking scripts, analytics platforms, or advertising networks within the extension.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: hello@clickument.co.uk
